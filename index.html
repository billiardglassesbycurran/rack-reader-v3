How to Use Rack Reader
📷 Camera & Scanning
📷
Start Camera
Activates your camera. Point at the pool table from above or at a playing angle.
⚡
Auto-Scan
When enabled, captures automatically when balls stop moving. Perfect for tripod use.
🎯
Scan Now
Manually captures the current camera frame instantly.
💡 Tripod tip: Mount phone overhead, set sensitivity to 5, still delay to 1.2s. App scans automatically every time balls stop.
🤖 AI Detection
🤖
AI Detect Button
After capturing a frame, tap to automatically find and mark balls. AI markers show a purple "AI" label.
📊
AI Confidence Slider
Low (10-30%) detects more balls. High (60%+) only places very confident markers.
💡 Best results: Shoot from above in good lighting. If AI misses balls, lower the confidence slider or mark manually.
🎱 Marking Balls
⚪
Cue Ball
Mark the white ball first. The app draws aim lines FROM the cue ball to your target.
🔴
Solid & Stripe
Mark your object balls. The app picks the closest one to the cue ball as primary target.
🕳️
Target Pocket
Mark where you want to pocket the ball. Unlocks cut angle, aim lines and ghost ball.
↩
Undo & Clear
Undo removes last marker. Clear removes all markers. X discards the frame entirely.
💡 Workflow: Mark Cue Ball → Target Ball → Target Pocket → Suggest Best Shot.
🎱 English / Spin Control
🔛
Enable in Settings
Toggle on English/Spin Control — the popup opens immediately so you can set your contact point right away.
🎱
Tap the Cue Ball Diagram
A cue ball diagram pops up with 6 zones: Follow (top), Draw (bottom), Stun (just below center), Left English, Right English, and Center Ball. Tap any zone to select it.
⚪
Tap On Canvas Too
Once you've placed the cue ball marker on the canvas, tap it (while in any mode other than Cue) to reopen the spin popup anytime.
➡️
Path Line on Canvas
After marking cue ball and target, a colored path line shows where the cue ball will travel after contact based on your spin selection.
🔍 Shot Suggestions
📐
Cut Angle
0° = straight in (easiest). 90° = maximum cut (hardest). Color coded green/amber/red.
🎯
Difficulty Rating
1-10 score combining cut angle, distance and spin. 1-3 Easy · 4-6 Moderate · 7-10 Difficult.
🛡️
Safety Suggestion
When difficulty hits 7+, the app automatically suggests a defensive safety play.
🎮
Game Modes
8-Ball targets your group. 9-Ball always hits lowest ball first. Straight Pool focuses on cluster control.
💡 Pro tip: Always mark the Target Pocket for the most accurate advice.
🔔 Sound Chimes
🔔
Two-Chime System
Soft click = table scanned. Louder two-tone = suggestion ready. Designed for tripod use so you hear when to look up.
